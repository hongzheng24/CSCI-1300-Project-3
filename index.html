Introduction

In this project, we design a website for Vendoo, a startup aimed at easing the lives of online sellers by centralizing online marketplaces, like Ebay and Poshmark, all onto one website. We will sketch, choose a design, create a low-fidelity wireframe, create high-fidelity mockups, receive critiques on our design, and conduct user testing.
